Abstract method implemented by the inherited classes to determine if a given piece can move to a new location on the board. The new location may have a piece from the opposing team that can be captured, or be an empty space. Pieces may jump over intermediate spaces like the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_knight}{Knight}}, or have to travel through the path to the new location for the other pieces and the path must be empty.

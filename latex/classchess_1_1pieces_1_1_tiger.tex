\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_tiger}{Tiger}} class is for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_tiger}{Tiger}} custom chess piece. \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_tiger}{Tiger}} pieces can jump to any space that is 2 spaces away horizontally or vertically. They can also move to any adjacent tile if there is an enemy piece there to capture it. If there is no piece at the chosen adjacent tile, then the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_tiger}{Tiger}} cannot move there.

Used to undo the last recorded move. This method can be used multiple times consecutively to undo the last moves for the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1_board}{Board}}, alternatives between sides and resets the turn to the original player if the undo button is called after a player has used their turn. Called by the undo button, will replace the pieces\textquotesingle{} original positions and display any captured pieces and restore board to the state it was in before the move.

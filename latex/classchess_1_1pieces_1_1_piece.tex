\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Abstract class that represents each Chess piece that is present on the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1_board}{Board}}. Extended by \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_pawn}{Pawn}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_rook}{Rook}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_knight}{Knight}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_bishop}{Bishop}}, \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_queen}{Queen}}, and \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_king}{King}}. The logic for the individual pieces and movement is implemented within each child class.

Abstract method implemented by the inherited classes to determine if a given piece can move to a new location on the board. The new location may have a piece from the opposing team that can be captured, or be an empty space. Pieces may jump over intermediate spaces like the \mbox{\hyperlink{classchess_1_1pieces_1_1_knight}{Knight}}, or have to travel through the path to the new location for the other pieces and the path must be empty.

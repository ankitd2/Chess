Detailed Description

The Tiger class is for the Tiger custom chess piece. Tiger pieces can jump to any space that is 2 spaces away horizontally or vertically. They can also move to any adjacent tile if there is an enemy piece there to capture it. If there is no piece at the chosen adjacent tile, then the Tiger cannot move there.

Abstract method implemented by the Tiger class with the logic for Tiger pieces. Tiger pieces can move to spaces that are a distance of 2 away from the current square in the horizontal or vertical directions. The tiger can also move to an adjacent square (including adjacent diagonal tile) if there is an enemy piece there to be captured.
